The all-in-one digital workspace for modern perfumers
From formula creation to ingredient sourcing – Perfumers Vault 2 streamlines your entire perfumery workflow. Built for indie creators, brands, and labs who demand precision and creativity.

Advanced formulation

Group by note type

Effortlessly organize your formula by note type—top, heart, base, or solvents—for clearer composition and easier adjustments.

Quickly see IFRA compliance for every ingredient and formula, ensuring safety and regulatory alignment as you create.

Stock view

Monitor ingredient stock levels in real time, get low-inventory alerts, and plan purchases directly from your workspace.

Generate QR codes for your formulas or ingredient lists to quickly share or access them on any device.
