Getting Started

Perfumers Vault 2 is currently available for iOS devices via the Apple App Store. We are working on bringing the app to Android and other platforms.

Coming Soon: Android version is in development and will be available soon.

Getting started is easy! Simply download the app from the App Store and follow these steps:

  1. Import the IFRA library for compliance checking
  2. Start adding your ingredients to build your inventory
  3. Create your first formula using our intuitive formula builder
  4. Explore advanced features like PV AI assistant

Absolutely! Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. All your data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers or any third parties for storage.

Local Data Storage

  • • All formulas, ingredients, and personal data stay on your device
  • • Optional iCloud sync uses your private, encrypted iCloud account
  • • No servers, no external databases, no third-party storage
  • • You maintain complete control over your data

When you enable iCloud sync, your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account - we cannot access it. Read our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Features & Usage

PV AI is your intelligent assistant powered by Google Gemini AI that helps with formula creation, ingredient suggestions, and perfumery guidance. PV AI can:

  • Suggest complementary ingredients for your formulas
  • Provide creative inspiration and formula modifications
  • Help with IFRA compliance questions
  • Offer technical guidance on perfumery techniques
  • Analyze your formulas and suggest improvements

Account Requirement

To use PV AI features, you'll need to have a Google account and agree to Google's terms of service. The AI processing is handled by Google's Gemini AI service.

Note: PV AI is an optional feature - you can use all other app features without any external accounts.

  • Export formulas to PDF with detailed formatting
  • Generate QR codes for quick formula sharing
  • Export ingredient lists and inventory data

Our IFRA compliance system automatically checks your formulas against the latest IFRA standards. The app includes an up-to-date IFRA library and provides:

  • Real-time compliance validation as you build formulas
  • Category-specific limit checking for different product types
  • Detailed restriction information for each ingredient
  • IFRA document generation
  • Regular library updates to maintain current standards
