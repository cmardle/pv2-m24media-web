This policy applies to Perfumers Vault 2, developed by Perfodyne Labs LTD.

Perfumers Vault 2 ("we", "our", "the App"), developed by Perfodyne Labs LTD, is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data when you use the Perfumers Vault 2 app except anonymous telemetry data automatically collected by Apple and sent to us regarding basic app usage information.

CloudKit and iCloud

Perfumers Vault 2 uses Apple CloudKit to sync data between your Apple devices (iPhone, iPad and Mac). Your data is stored in a private database, which is encrypted with an account-based key. Therefore, we do not have access to any data stored in your iCloud account.

We also do not have access to your Apple ID - only a unique identifier that is scoped to just our app. It is not possible to track your identity between apps with this identifier. We do not have access to the data you have stored in your Perfumers Vault 2 CloudKit container.

The only data we have access to regarding your use of Perfumers Vault 2 are crash reports and anonymised telemetry data sent to us by Apple. We do not share or sell this data.

To delete data stored in your private CloudKit container, use the settings app on your device. This will wipe all of your Perfumers Vault 2 data across all devices upon syncing.

For more information visit Apple's privacy policy.

No account or login required

The App does not require or provide any account creation or login. You can use the App without registering or signing in.

Subscription Management (RevenueCat)

Perfumers Vault 2 uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat is a third-party service that helps us process subscription data and manage user entitlements across platforms.

When you make a subscription purchase, RevenueCat may collect and process certain information related to your purchase, including:

  • Your Apple ID (in an anonymized form)
  • Purchase receipts and transaction details
  • Subscription status and entitlement information
  • Device and app version information

This data is used solely for subscription management, validation, and providing you with access to premium features. RevenueCat does not have access to your personal information stored within the Perfumers Vault 2 app, such as your formulas, ingredients, or notes.

Third-Party Subscription Service

For detailed information about how RevenueCat handles subscription data, please review their policies:

  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Terms of Service
  • RevenueCat GDPR Compliance

You can manage your subscription directly through your Apple ID account settings. RevenueCat processes subscription data in accordance with Apple's App Store guidelines and their own privacy policies listed above.

AI Features (PV AI)

When you use PV AI features, your queries and formula data are processed by Google's Gemini AI to provide intelligent suggestions and guidance. Please refer to the links below for how Google manages that data. We do not use your data to train AI models or share it with other users.

Third-Party AI Service

By using PV AI features, you agree to Google's terms and privacy policies:

  • Google Terms of Service
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Gemini API Additional Terms of Service

You can choose to disable AI features at any time in the app settings if you prefer not to use external AI services.

PubChem Integration

Perfumers Vault 2 integrates with PubChem, a free chemistry database maintained by the National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI), to provide chemical information and molecular data for ingredients. When you use PubChem search features, your queries are sent to PubChem's servers to retrieve chemical data.

This integration allows you to:

  • Search for chemical compounds and their properties
  • Access molecular structure information
  • Retrieve safety and regulatory data
  • View chemical identifiers and synonyms

Third-Party Database Service

PubChem is operated by the U.S. National Library of Medicine. For information about their data policies:

  • NCBI Website and Data Usage Policies
  • NLM Web Policies
  • HHS Privacy Policy

When using PubChem features, your search queries are transmitted to PubChem's servers. We do not store or have access to your PubChem search history. All interactions with PubChem are subject to their terms of use and privacy policies.

IFRA Integration

Perfumers Vault 2 integrates with the International Fragrance Association (IFRA) website to provide automatic import of fragrance ingredient safety standards and usage guidelines. This feature allows you to stay up-to-date with the latest IFRA standards and regulations.

The IFRA integration supports:

  • Auto-import of IFRA standards and amendments
  • Ingredient usage restrictions and limitations
  • Category-specific concentration limits
  • Allergen declarations and requirements
  • Regulatory compliance information

Third-Party Regulatory Organization

When using IFRA auto-import features, data is retrieved from IFRA's official website. For information about their policies:

  • IFRA Privacy Policy
  • IFRA Terms and Conditions
  • IFRA Standards Library

Important Notice

The Auto import feature is removed in version 1.0.5. Users will need to manually import IFRA standards after this update.

When you use the auto-import feature, requests are made to IFRA's website to retrieve the latest standards and regulatory information. We do not store or have access to your IFRA browsing history. All data retrieved is stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud account.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal or behavioral data with Perfodyne Labs LTD or third parties. We only receive anonymous crash reports and telemetry data directly from Apple to help improve stability and usability. These reports contain purely technical details such as device type, operating system version, and crash logs.

No Third-Party Analytics

All third-party analytics have been permanently removed to ensure full GDPR compliance and enhance user privacy.

What this means for you:

  • No usage analytics or telemetry data is collected by third parties
  • No personal or behavioral data is transmitted outside Apple’s ecosystem
  • Enhanced privacy protection in line with GDPR requirements
  • Only Apple’s optional, anonymous crash reporting remains

Crash Reporting: We may receive anonymous crash reports directly from Apple’s crash reporting service, which helps us identify and resolve technical issues. This data:

  • Contains no personally identifiable information
  • Is processed entirely within Apple’s ecosystem
  • Includes only technical crash details (e.g. stack traces, device type, iOS version)
  • Can be disabled in your iOS privacy settings
