Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your Pattr account

Important Information

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed from our systems.

Method 1: Delete from the App

If you have access to your Pattr account, you can delete it directly from the app:

  1. 1. Open the Pattr app on your device
  2. 2. Navigate to your Profile
  3. 3. Go to Settings
  4. 4. Select Delete Account
  5. 5. Follow the prompts to confirm your account deletion

Method 2: Email Request

If you don't have access to your account or are unable to delete it through the app, you can request account deletion by email:

Email Account Deletion Request

Send an email to admin@pattr.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"

Include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your Pattr account
  • Your username (if you remember it)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
  • Confirmation that you understand the deletion is permanent

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • • All personal identifying information will be permanently removed
  • • Your profile and account data will be deleted from our servers
  • • Your posts and content may remain on the platform but will be anonymized (no longer linked to your identity)
  • • This action cannot be undone - you'll need to create a new account if you want to use Pattr again

Need Help?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process or need assistance, please contact our support team at admin@pattr.app. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
